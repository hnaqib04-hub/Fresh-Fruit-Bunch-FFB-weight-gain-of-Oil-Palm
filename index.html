Oil Palm FFB Weight Gain Simulator

Scenario: In Malaysian Agrotech, tracking the Fresh Fruit Bunch (FFB) weight is critical for yield prediction. Using realistic data trends for Elaeis guineensis, we model the weight $f(x)$ in kilograms over 22 weeks of development.

By calculating the derivative $f'(x)$, we find the Daily Accumulation Rate. This tells us when the fruit is bulking most aggressively.

Growth Function: $f(x) = -0.004x^3 + 0.12x^2 + 0.4x + 2$
Derivative (Rate): $f'(x) = -0.012x^2 + 0.24x + 0.4$
